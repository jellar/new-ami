What is the FirstCare Service?

The FirstCare Service is an innovative approach to helping organisations manage and reduce absence. There are 5 main components to it:

24/7 Absence Recording and Notifications

FirstCare provides each client with a dedicated absence line which rings into our specialist London based 24/7 Absence Contact Centre. Here, we record the details of every absence in a fair, consistent, complete and accurate manner while at the same time offering medical advice and support to employees. Following each call, our system will issue timely notifications to all key stakeholders, providing detailed absence information and guidance on the best next steps to take and when.

24/7 Online Management Information>

Details of every absence appear in real-time reports on MyFirstCare a secure online portal available to HR and Line Managers and providing dashboards, trend reports, focus lists and task monitoring reports, enabling users to proactively manage absence and make informed decisions.

Absence Policy Alerts and Wellbeing Referral Triggers

FirstCare map the triggers of an organisation's absence policy on to its system and scan each and every absence before generating alerts to relevant stakeholders, such as line managers, HR, Health and Safety Officers and Occupational Health. These alerts can be fully customised with bespoke content and attachments to ensure the right steps are taken to investigate them.

Return to Work Interview Monitoring

FirstCare track the online completion of all Return to Work Interviews and produce reporting on compliance throughout the organisation by both hierarchy area and absence reason to help identify areas of non-compliance. In addition, FirstCare provide dynamic RTW forms, where the questions are driven by the absence reason, and a host of supporting material for managers to use in the interviews.

Account Management

FirstCare assigns an Account Management team to each of its clients. This team comprises of a Senior Account Manager and 2 Service Delivery Advisors, who work with our clients key contacts to analyse absence trends, recommend best practice adjustments to policies and procedures, and discuss the best ways to manage and reduce absence.
